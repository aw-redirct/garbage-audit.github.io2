Valley Park MS Garbage Audit

Programmed & Designed by Yasser’s Group

Plan

Our plan is to spread awareness about misplacing garbage, to create solutions for this issue, so that both you and I can live in a safer, healthier abode.

Action

We always do our best to become an eco-friendly school, so we created this to keep track of our schools garbage to maintain a healthier enviorment.

Goals

The reason for the VPMS Garbage Audit is so we can track our garbage to keep our hygene flawless, inorder to reach our goal of becoming the best eco-friendly school.

Findings

Our class (7B Baetz) arranged a garbage audit and this what we discovered

86% of food waste is not garbage and 14% is recyclable containers and from our results the average student creates 67LB of waste each year

FACTS!!!

DID YOU KNOW THAT...

Recycling one aluminum can saves enough energy to run a TV for three hours — or the equivalent of a half a gallon of gasoline. Motor oil never wears out, it just gets dirty. Old oil can be recycled and used again. It takes 80-100 years for an aluminum can to decompose in a landfill.
